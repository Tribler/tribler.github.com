How it works



Tribler is a software program that facilitates safe filesharing.

The files are shared by use of the Tribler software which means that there is no download website necessary. Users can choose files that they want to download to their computers and select files that they want to share with other users. The files are shared between users and there is no central computer that provides every file to all users. Tribler uses Torrents to distributes files. A Torrent describes the file content and is required for downloading to start.

The basics

Search for content

To find the content you would like to download type in a keyword in the search bar. The more specific your search is, the more likely you are to get the result you're looking for.

Download content

Before downloading files onto your computer first a Torrent file, decribing the content of the file, must be downloaded. Tribler can go ahead and find the Torrent file of your first results, to speed up the downloading process.

Consume content

Because of the integrated video and audio player you can almost immediately start watching your favorite video(s) or listen to your favorite song(s).

Extra options

Filter the content found

You can filter the results from you search by adding keywords to the filter box. You can also sort the content, or set a "size limit" to determine which results are shown.

Uploading

Tribler is a social application. This means you can make can show to everyone what you like and dislike. And by sharing your content you also help other Tribler users to enjoy their favorite content.

Channels

Channels are collection of torrents managed by a particular user. By finding users with similar taste you can find content that you might find interesting.You can add channels as Favorites to aid your search.

MyChannel

You can also create your own Channel, to make it easier for other users, which have similar interests to find the content you are willing to share.
