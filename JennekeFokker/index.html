Jenneke Fokker

In September 2004 Jenneke started on a Ph.D. project called Inducing Human Cooperation in Peer-to-Peer Systems. Believing that users are the decisive factor for the success of Tribler, she investigates what (social) psychological phenomena could stimulate users to engage voluntarily and massively in specific forms of cooperative behavior.
