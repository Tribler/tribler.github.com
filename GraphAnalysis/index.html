Tribler/BitTorrent vs. topology/graph/modeling research

Tribler is build on advances in many areas of research. One of the more complicated and least understood areas is that of graph theory. We believe a better understanding of the graphs inside Tribler could be exploited and lead to superior performance.

The graphs inside Tribler are present at various levels. The trust between people, the similarity of taste between people, the Internet connectivity fabric between people, etc.
