GUI design


Tribler is equally based on two fundamental concepts: the content accessible through Tribler, and the users of Tribler. For each content element and user, a large amount of information is available, which can be categorized in:

  • information for filtering the displayed content or users (best rated, only videos, most popular, etc.);
  • information for browsing from one element (either content or user) to the other (for downloading content similar to an already downloaded item, or for checking the latest downloads of your friends, etc.).
