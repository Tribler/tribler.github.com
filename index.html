Moving towards overcoming censorship


Towards Anonymity

Mass-surveillance and government snooping is becoming increasingly impossible with Tribler. We're trying to combine Bittorrent streaming with the Tor hidden services architecture. A lot of work goes into making this feature stable.

Streaming

Tribler has an intergrated videoplayer which allows you to stream videos even before the download is finished. The videoplayer is fully functional and includes the ability to seek within a video, without having to download the entire file first.
