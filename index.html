Overcoming Censorship

Tribler aims to liberate the media by not relying on websites, severs or DNS infrastructure. Stay in control of your own content. Find share and enjoy, whenever you want!

Towards Anonimity

Mass-surveillance and government snooping is becoming increasingly impossible with Tribler. We're trying to combine Bittorrent streaming with the Tor hidden services architecture. A lot of work goes into making this feature stable.

How it works

Downloading

Tribler is an application that enables its users to find, enjoy and share content. With content we mean video, audio, pictures, and much more.

Get started

No websites

Tribler software facilitates sharing content without an external server. You can choose to download files without having to search the web for downloadsites.

Open Source

Triblers software is Open Source so you can verify if it works as advertised.
