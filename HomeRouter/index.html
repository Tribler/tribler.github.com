50 Euro for your old home router

Do you have an old home router lying around collecting dust?

Delft University of Technology is giving 50 Euro for home routers. For special symmetric NAT boxes we even give you 150 Euro. Just drop by in the town of Delft on X aug or Y sep and we hand over the cash (between 10:00 and 19:00).

The reason behind gathering old hardware is that we want to re-create the real Internet for our peer-to-peer research. With these boxes in our lab and connected to an old supercomputer we can move beyond the speed of Bittorrent.
