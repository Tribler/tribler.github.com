Community Measurements Paper

We identified a set of experiments to be performed for six different trackers. The overall goal is to compare reliability and performance for trackers with different properties and different levels of closedness.

Trackers

The chosen trackers are:

  • Denis public tracker
  • PirateBay
  • Demonoid
  • TVTorrents
  • Polish tracker
  • SceneTorrents
  • Sumotorrent

Experiments

  1. Lifetime of seeders (Mihai)
  2. Size vs. performance (Mihai)
  3. Connectability (Lucia)
  4. Connectability vs. performance (Lucia)
  5. (Geospreading (Maciek): not useful)
  6. Seeder vs. leecher traffic (Maciek)
